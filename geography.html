Geography


The darker the state, the better it is doing in terms of job performance compared to other states. The standarized proportions will be given a different range dependent on the data for the month. Therefore it is important to look at the value in comparison to the range and darkness. Midwest was consistently doing better than the rest of the US until month 9, when states evened out.

In general you can see that all states had significantly larger job availability in 2021 than 2020. Almost every state has 1.5x more job availability in 2021 than 2020.

The Hospitality and Entertainment industries were some of the most negatively affected industries from COVID in 2020 and 2021. It can be seen that the entirety of the US was affected negatively in the Entertainment industry. For the hospitality industry, California, North-West states, and North-East states were performing better than the rest of the country.
